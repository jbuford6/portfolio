About Jamaris Buford

I started out as a student athlete at Vestavia Hills High School, I'm the small one wearing #6.

High School

I learned a lot of good things in High School, one of the most important things i still use today is my work ethic to be the best at what i do. As I attended High School I also worked at Western Suppermarket down the hill from my school. So after practice, from my Sophmore to Senior year, I would shower and change into my work clothes, walk down the hill and clock-in.

After High-School, I was blessed with an athletic scholarship to Miles College, here's a picture from signing day, still the small one but with the purple hat.

College

Being a student athlete in college was much different than high school. From 4:30 a.m. practices to 3-A-Days in the hot sun, it all taught me how to focus on what was really important. I learned quickly how to manage my time accordingly, prioritize things that needed to be done, and most of all do as much as I could in one day.
